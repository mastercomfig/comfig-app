* Updated the Ullapool Caber:
  * Current beta design remains, with the following changes/tweaks:
  * Taking lethal self-damage from the Caber will now reduce your respawn time by 5 seconds (to a minimum of a 2 second respawn)
  * If the Caber deals no damage to any enemies, it will not break the caber and will not deal full self-damage to the user. Misses in this way will still spawn an explosion, even if nothing at all was hit (i.e airbursts), and will not interrupt Shield Charging.
  * The Caber will now detonate properly against hit surfaces (i.e striking a wall or the ground), while still detonating around the user if striking an enemy with it
