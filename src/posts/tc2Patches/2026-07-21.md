* We've updated how tiebreakers and the match timer interact to make match time more predictable and not drag out.
  * Work will continue in this area to make match time even less variable and less exhausting, as this is a big point of feedback we have heard.

* Casual match timer update:
  * If there are only 5 minutes or less left in a match when a series ends, and the teams are not tied, the match will now end rather than continuing on to a new series.
  * If time runs out while playing a series, and 1 team is ahead in the series win condition, they will be awarded the win.
  * If the teams are tied during this time, or while playing a tiebreaker series, then whoever scores next will win the match.

* Fixed PASS Time not being considered a captures based gamemode for Casual matchmaking
* Fixed Administrator not announcing match time limit
* Fixed overheal on outline nameplates overflowing the health bar if overheal was above 200%
* Fixed Sappers not being named correctly in various parts of the HUD, including outline nameplates
* Simplified Teleporter Entrances to be named "Teleporter" in outline nameplates
