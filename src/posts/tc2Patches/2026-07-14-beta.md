* Reworked the Powerjack:
  * Dealing damage on any weapon and extinguishing teammates charges the Powerjack's battery
  * While active, the Powerjack drains the battery, giving a 20% speed boost while active
  * New attribute: Scrappy: the Powerjack restores +25 health and 25% ammo on kill
  * 20% damage vulnerability while active

* Reworked the Hot Hand:
  * Each slap charges up a Super Slap, taking 2 hits on burning players and 3 hits otherwise
  * The Super Slap powerfully knocks back and humiliates the victim, dealing mini-crits and applying Marked for Death for 5 seconds.
  * The target is ignited by the Super Slap, and the Pyro receives a 3 second speed boost.

* Enemy flag carriers now display their health on their outline
  * The held flag will show the appropriate team color of the carrier while stolen
