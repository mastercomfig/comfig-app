* Added Territorial Control 2 (TC2):
    * A 50v50 gamemode where teams fight over the entire territory on a Territorial Control map
    * Enabled with tf_tc2_mode 1
    * Support added for tc_hydro. Community maps can be updated to support the new gamemode
    * Spawn by selecting a deployment location: control points your team controls, or friendly teleporters
    * Teams must capture all other control points before sieging the enemy base. If the defending team is able to hold their base for 7 minutes, control of their other points is restored.
